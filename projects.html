Our Projects


Through three of its prestigious projects Ullas, GLL, and Soch, FEPSI tries to create a better society with individuals possessing confidence and the urge to contribute to the wellbeing of others.

With 7 successful years since its inception, FEPSI has earned a lot of respect for the work it does. The sincerity and the dedication of its members towards the theme of the club have been its impetus all along.

ULLAS

Ullas acts as the face of FEPSI. Therefore, when the events held by this project are successful, the whole crew gets off on the right foot. Holding outreaches which are essentially the cornerstone of this NGO has ensured that we uphold our goal which is to help those in need. As the years have passed, we have done nothing but find ways to refine the experiences we provide to those unacknowledged orphanages.

SOCH

The term “Soch” literally translates to “a thought”, which is one of the first steps to a revolution. All major changes and progressions in the world start with “a thought”. All leaders become great with “a thought”. SOCH is a project that lies at the heart of the club. The project puts together various social engagements with notable personalities in order to speak about various political and other contemporary issues across the world. SOCH prides itself on reaching out and trying to influence the general youth through vibrant thought-provoking VOYs(Voice of Youth sessions) that help to introduce and engage them in real world problems and how they can be solved.

GLL

GLL stands for global leadership labs. This project aims to inculcate and improve leadership qualities present in oneself. It is conducted as a semester long project. Various activities are involved under GLL which, with time, help the participants grow from within and recognize their strengths and weaknesses. Moreover, in this process of learning, students learn to overcome their weaknesses, become motivated and be much more confident about their goals.

ULLAS

Birthday Campaign

Celebrations are always sweeter when they are shared. Being a source of service to the people around us is the greatest gift we could give to society . This year, on your birthday, be the change you want to see. As a birthday gift, give yourself the ability to do something great. Set up a fundraiser to change somebody’s life for the better. Restore their sense of pride, self-sufficiency, and independence. We all have the luxury and money to spend on cakes, gifts, clothes, shoes, and a lot more . But remember, there are millions of people who don't have more than one pair of shoes or clothes, don't have a house to live in and do not possess enough money to celebrate their birthday. So we, the members of FEPSI, came up with the BIRTHDAY CAMPAIGN, which is an initiative to celebrate our birthdays with children and celebrate our happiness with theirs. You can donate whatever you feel like - food, clothes, books, money to witness a smile on these children's faces. We celebrate our birthday with the children, conduct a donation drive, collect items such as books, clothes, pencils, crayons, money, and donate them to the children.

Harita

An initiative called Haritha was birthed with the sole purpose of giving back to mother nature.We have incorporated this initiative in our outreaches as well. The first time we conducted Haritha was during our outreach to Sacred Heart Boarding School. To call it a success would be an understatement. Voluntary contributions are taken from club members and a few others by whom saplings are planted while in outreaches.This helps accomplish our goal to replenish mother nature’s rich reserves.

Library Campaign

All of us agree that every child deserves equal opportunities in life and there is no better way of doing this than by giving them the power of knowledge. So we at FEPSI have come up with an initiative termed "Library Campaign", where we are collecting English and Tamil books for primary and secondary school students to set up an entire library in their boarding school. We request you to donate your old books so that your little contribution can become someone's bundle of joy.

Mannat

Mannat is an initiative in which clothes are collected from our happiness stalls set up during Riviera, FEPSI’s logistics, and external sources as well. It was made sure that the clothes were provided to the needy and old people that can’t earn. Our mission is to improve the quality of life for the shelter-less to break the cycle of poverty. This event has brought smiles to the ones in need and to the volunteers as well.

Akshaya

The food donation drives held by Project Ullas allow us to do our part for the people around us by reducing their plight of hunger because we, at FEPSI believe that one of the ways to touch a person’s soul is through food. Akshaya is a series of food contributions initiated by Ullas. The objective of these food donation drives is to distribute food as a group or individually to the people who have very little access to proper nutrition. Sometimes we also join hands with local trusts and charities in order to get the maximum reach to the people in need. The core belief that we as Fepsians embody is to help and contribute as much as we can, wherever we are because “no one has ever become poor by giving”-Anne Frank.

SOCH

Voice Of Youth

SOCH’s Voice of Youth sessions aim to project the opinions of the youth, related to the ongoing events around the world through the platforms of social media and interaction in the form of interesting activities with the club’s core members. Various socio-political issues are taken up, and students are asked to convey their viewpoints to an audience in order to bring about healthy discussion and draft feasible solutions.

Video Shoots

SOCH’s video shoots highlight the point of view of the students across campus on various contemporary issues. We take up weighted topics that deserve to be addressed. Students are asked to answer a series of questions, in order to help them take a stand. These sessions are also conducted as video seminars in order to expand our audience. We expand these sessions to other colleges and NGOs across the country.

GLL

Leadership Labs

A quality that transcends vision into reality is “Leadership”. And the most important skill a leader possesses is to instill that excellence into real life. The GLL curriculum covers several events and experiences ranging from fun-filled activities to educational ones which help develop all the skills a leader should possess, within two semesters. Each event is an enriching experience that helps the participants to learn how to work in a team, improve their public speaking skills, and work on their creativity. The curriculum not only shapes the leaders of tomorrow but also plays a huge role in creating a vibrant and inquisitive society.
