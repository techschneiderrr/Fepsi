About Us


FEPSI is not just working as co-member


Forum for Empowering People through Social Innovation and entertainment, VIT is an NGO well known for its activities in and around Vellore and is known to all as FEPSI-VIT.It is a very famous NGO and boasts of extreme working ability which attributes to its popularity which it has earned in a very short span of time.

With a mere 6 years since its inception, FEPSI has earned a lot of respect for the work it does. The sincerity and the dedication of its members towards the theme of the club has been its impetus all along.
