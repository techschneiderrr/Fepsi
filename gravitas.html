Gravitas Events


Lets EMPOWER !!


GraVITas, the Techno-Management Extravaganza organized by VIT Vellore, is an internationally acclaimed technical fest. It aims to expose budding graduates to recent trends in various domains, challenging the young brains to solve technical problems while interacting with their peers. Among the plethora of events held, FEPSI is proud to present its four technical events namely Pitch Perfect, Socio-White Hack, Start-a-thon, and Smart India transformation.

Pitch Perfect
Do you have a knack of thinking outside the box?

Wish to showcase what separates you from the rest? Consider yourself a capable problem solver? We’ve got the perfect platform for you!

Pitch Perfect is an entrepreneurial think tank where participants are provided with tools to come up with solutions to a problem in a given domain. Through a series of rounds, participants are mentored and work towards refining their solution to be as effective and financially viable as possible. The participants are provided with various challenges and suggestions that help them understand the path that needs to be discovered when one decides to establish a startup. Pitch Perfect provides the perfect platform for all the students who aim to become entrepreneurs.

Socio White Hack
Socio-white hack was one of our 3 flagship events. We say this with great exuberance that the event was a resounding success. The theme of our event, Ethical hacking, has proved to be helpful for all ages. The event has proved to be a great success with active participation from our viewers. The speaker has provided great insights into the domain and has helped the participants throughout the event. We hope to have this kind of success in all of our upcoming events as well.

Smart India Transformation
Smart India Transformation was one of the most successful events in Gravitas19. The event has concentrated on solving various social issues with solutions that have proved to be practical. Each group was given a city and various positions such as financial planner, designer, mayor, etc. Each city was given an agenda such as ‘smart living’, ‘smart healthcare’, ‘smart agriculture’ and is asked to research and develop an idea in alignment to the geographical needs of their city. They were also provided with a crisis such as ‘climate change’, ‘political unrest’ etc, and were required to renovate their ideas accordingly. Saying that the cities ensured sustainable development would be an understatement.

Start-A-Thon
Every successful business started small, and none faced a smooth path.

Start-a-thon was one of FEPSI's leading events during Gravitas 2020. With an aim to attract young budding entrepreneurs, Start-a-thon provided them with a virtual platform where they could come forth and build upon their ideas, experiencing real-life hardships while simultaneously honing their skills to venture into the business realm. With an esteemed panel of judges and a plethora of domains, startup ventures from various fields left everyone impressed with their creativity and their grasp of solving real-world problems. With the unimaginable response and support that it got, to call it a success would be an understatement.
